How to Play Bruin Catch

🐻 Move the Bruin: Use Left/Right Arrow keys or Drag with your mouse/finger.

🌟 Catch Items:

  • 🎓 📚 🏀 🏆: Good items! Score points!
  • 🌟 (Golden): Bonus points!
  • 💎 (Diamond): Ultra bonus points!
  • 💙 (Life): Gain an extra life!
  • 🍄 (Grow): Bruin gets bigger temporarily!
  • 💧 (Shrink): Bruin gets smaller temporarily!
  • 🧲 (Magnet): Attracts good items for a bit!
  • ⏳ (Slow-Mo): Slows down items for a bit!

⚠️ Avoid Bad Items:

  • 🗑️ 💥 💔: These have red sparkles! Avoid them or lose a life!

🔥 Combos: Catch multiple good items in a row for score multipliers!

🚀 Level Up: Score points to reach higher levels. The game gets faster!

🎉 Bonus Rounds: Extra valuable items might appear!
